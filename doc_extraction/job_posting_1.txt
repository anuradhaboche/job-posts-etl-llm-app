Senior Data Engineer — Stripe (Remote / San Francisco, CA)

About the role:
Stripe is looking for a Senior Data Engineer to join our Data Platform team.
You'll be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the data infrastructure
that powers our analytics, reporting, and machine learning capabilities.

What you'll do:
- Design and build scalable data pipelines processing billions of events daily
- Maintain and improve our real-time streaming infrastructure using Kafka and Flink
- Partner with ML teams to build and maintain feature pipelines
- Own data quality and observability across our warehouse

Requirements:
- 6+ years of data engineering experience
- Strong Python and SQL skills
- Deep experience with Spark or Flink for large-scale data processing
- Experience with Kafka or similar streaming systems
- Experience with a cloud data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift)
- Familiarity with dbt for analytics engineering

Nice to have:
- Experience with LLM pipelines or RAG architectures
- Experience with Airflow or similar orchestration tools
- Contributions to open-source data projects

Compensation:
$175,000 – $220,000 base salary
Equity + comprehensive benefits

Employment type: Full-time
Seniority: Senior
Remote: Yes (US timezones preferred)
